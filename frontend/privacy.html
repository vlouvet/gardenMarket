Privacy Policy

1. Information we collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: email address, password (stored as a one-way hash), display name, and the role you select (buyer or grower).
  • Marketplace activity: listings you create or interact with, items you add to a cart, orders you place, and pickup history at distribution centers.
  • Grower content: photos, posts, sensor readings, and other information you upload through the grower dashboard.
  • Payment data: processed by [Stripe]. We store the minimum necessary to complete and reconcile a transaction (e.g. last-four digits, tokenized identifier, billing zip). We do not store full card numbers.
  • Technical data: IP address, device, browser, and aggregated traffic data. We use a privacy-friendly analytics provider (Plausible) that does not set cross-site tracking cookies.

2. How we use it

  • To operate the marketplace, including matching orders to growers and pickup centers.
  • To process payments and refunds.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, or violations of our Terms of Service.
  • To send transactional notifications (order updates, account actions). You can manage marketing communication preferences in your profile.
  • To improve the platform through aggregated analytics.

3. How we share it

  • Other users: growers see the buyer name and pickup window for orders involving their listings; buyers see grower display names and listing details. We do not share contact information beyond what is necessary to fulfill an order.
  • Service providers: hosting (cloud provider), payment processing (Stripe), analytics (Plausible), and email delivery. These providers are bound by contract to use the data only for the services they provide to us.
  • Legal requirements: we may disclose information where required by law, valid legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of GardenMarket or its users.
  • Business transfers: if GardenMarket is acquired or reorganized, your information may be transferred subject to this Privacy Policy or a successor policy with comparable protections.

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Cookies and similar technologies

We use a small number of strictly-necessary cookies to keep you logged in (the JWT access token is stored in browser local storage rather than a cookie). Plausible analytics is cookie-free. Third-party scripts are not loaded for advertising purposes.

5. Retention

  • Account information is retained for the life of the account.
  • Order and payment records are retained for [7] years to satisfy tax and audit requirements.
  • Listings, posts, and photos are retained until you delete them or close your account, after which they are removed from public view; backups are purged on the regular backup rotation.
  • Server logs are retained for [90] days for security and operational troubleshooting.

6. Your choices

  • You can review and update most account information from the profile page.
  • You can request a copy of your data or deletion of your account by emailing privacy@gardenmarket.local.
  • Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights (access, correction, portability, deletion, objection, restriction). We will honor verifiable requests within the timeframes required by applicable law.

7. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including encryption in transit (TLS), password hashing, role-based access controls, and regular dependency updates. No system is perfectly secure; if you believe an account has been compromised, contact us immediately at security@gardenmarket.local.

8. Children

GardenMarket is not directed to children under 13 (or the higher age defined by your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has registered, contact us and we will delete the account.

9. International transfers

Our infrastructure is hosted in [region]. If you access GardenMarket from elsewhere, your data will be transferred to and processed in that region under appropriate safeguards.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced through the platform or by email. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

11. Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to privacy@gardenmarket.local.
