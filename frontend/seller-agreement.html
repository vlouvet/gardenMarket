This Seller Agreement (the “Agreement”) sets out the terms between GardenMarket and individuals or entities that list and sell items on the platform (each, a “Grower”). It supplements, and is incorporated into, the GardenMarket Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Capitalized terms not defined here have the meaning given in the Terms of Service.

1. Eligibility and grower onboarding

  • You must be at least 18 years old and legally able to enter contracts.
  • You must have the right to sell every item you list and to operate as a producer in your jurisdiction.
  • We may require identity verification, business documentation, or insurance certificates before activating grower features.
  • We may pause or revoke grower access at any time for violations of this Agreement, the Terms of Service, or applicable law.

2. Listings and accuracy

  • You are solely responsible for the accuracy and legality of each listing, including plant or species identification, growing methods, organic certification claims, allergen disclosures, and pricing.
  • You agree not to list items you cannot lawfully sell or items that infringe intellectual-property rights.
  • Photographs must be your own or licensed for use, and must accurately depict the item being sold.
  • We may remove listings that are misleading, unsafe, non-compliant, or violate platform standards. Repeated violations can result in suspension or termination.

3. Food safety, labeling, and compliance

  • You are responsible for complying with all food-safety, agricultural, labeling, weights-and-measures, and other regulations applicable to your products and your jurisdiction.
  • You agree not to list items subject to recalls, prohibited additives, or restricted plant-pest categories.
  • If you make claims that require certification (e.g., organic, biodynamic, USDA program), you must hold and maintain the underlying certification and provide proof on request.
  • You must notify GardenMarket promptly if you become aware of a safety incident, recall, or regulatory action affecting any item you have listed.

4. Order fulfillment and pickup

  • You agree to accept and prepare confirmed orders by the agreed pickup window, deliver items to the assigned distribution center on time, and notify the platform if you cannot fulfill an order.
  • Items must be packaged appropriately for the product type, including any required temperature handling.
  • Orders that are repeatedly canceled, delivered late, or fail quality checks at the distribution center may affect your visibility, eligibility for promotional placement, or grower standing.

5. Pricing, fees, and payouts

  • You set the price for each listing. Prices must include any applicable taxes you are required to collect; the platform does not collect or remit taxes on your behalf unless explicitly stated.
  • GardenMarket charges a marketplace fee on each completed transaction. Current fees are disclosed on the grower dashboard. We may change fees with at least [30] days’ notice.
  • Payouts are made through our payment provider [Stripe] on the schedule shown in the dashboard, subject to identity verification and banking information being on file.
  • You are responsible for chargebacks, refunds caused by your error or non-fulfillment, and any taxes owed on your earnings.

6. Refunds, returns, and disputes

  • Buyers may request a refund or replacement for items that are missing, materially different from the listing, spoiled on arrival, or otherwise non-conforming, within the window described at checkout.
  • You will respond to refund requests in good faith. Where a refund is appropriate, the platform may issue it from your pending payout balance and notify you.
  • If a dispute cannot be resolved between you and the buyer, GardenMarket will make a final decision based on available evidence, which both parties agree to accept for purposes of platform handling.

7. Independent contractor

Nothing in this Agreement creates an employment, partnership, joint venture, or agency relationship between you and GardenMarket. You are an independent operator and are responsible for your own taxes, registrations, insurance, and operating obligations.

8. Insurance and indemnification

  • We may require you to carry and maintain commercially reasonable product-liability insurance and to provide proof on request.
  • You agree to defend, indemnify, and hold GardenMarket harmless from claims arising out of items you sell, content you post, or your breach of this Agreement, except to the extent caused by GardenMarket’s gross negligence or willful misconduct.

9. Intellectual property

You retain ownership of listings, photos, and other content you upload. You grant GardenMarket the license described in the Terms of Service to host, display, and promote that content for the operation of the platform.

10. Term and termination

  • This Agreement remains in effect while you have an active grower account.
  • You may close your grower account at any time. Pending orders must be fulfilled or refunded before account closure.
  • We may suspend or terminate grower access immediately for material breaches, fraud, repeated quality failures, or where required by law.
  • Provisions that by their nature should survive termination (payment, indemnification, limitation of liability, dispute resolution) will survive.

11. Changes

We may update this Agreement from time to time. Material changes will be announced through the grower dashboard or by email. Continued use of grower features after the effective date of an update constitutes acceptance of the updated Agreement.

12. Contact

Grower-relations questions can be sent to growers@gardenmarket.local.
